# title: About
---
Hi! I’m Audhi, and welcome to my blog. Here, I write about topics like data simulation, machine learning, ETL, Bayesian modeling, and large language models. I share ideas on how to turn complex data into useful results and build models to understand real-world problems.

I am also interested in traceability in supply chains and how data can make processes more clear. Sometimes, I share experiments, talk about technical challenges, or write about interesting trends in technology and data.

Connect with me on LinkedIn: [@audhiaprilliant](https://id.linkedin.com/in/audhiaprilliant){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}
